  • Summoner's Rift

    The Summoner's Rift is the most commonly used Field of Justice.The map was given a graphical and technical update on May 23rd, 2012 and remade from scratch on November 12th, 2014.

  • All Random All Mid

    ARAM (also known as All Random, All Mid) is a single lane game mode. It gives you a random champion, with options to randomly re-roll for another champion.

  • Twisted Treeline

    The Twisted Treeline is the second classic League of Legends map. It typically features faster, bloodier matches with an emphasis on ganking and small-scale skirmishes.
